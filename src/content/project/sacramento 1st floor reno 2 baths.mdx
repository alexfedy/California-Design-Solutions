## SACRAMENTO 1ST FLOOR RENO - 2 BATHS & LAUNDRY RECONFIGURE

This project dramatically reconfigured walls in the bathrooms, hall and laundry rooms. Odd angles & strange shapes were maddening to our Clients - they disliked the space so much they began removing the sheetrock to reveal new layout options! Together we found a plan that functioned beautifully for our Clients and met all requirements, including their 2 beautiful Basenji pups! Only two tones were used throughout for a peaceful modern vibe - beautiful quartzite counters and rich wood graining set the 2 tone palette.

Project Year: 2021

Project Cost: $75,001 - $100,000

Country: United States
