## KRAUS PROJECT-Designed for a Quick Home Sale

Every inch of oak cabinetry within this home was painted in a rich chocolate stain, saving the client thousands vs. the cost for new cabinetry! The oak handrails & spindles, vanities, hall linen cabinet doors were all enhanced by paint, showcasing the beautiful oak grain. New appliances, split face quartz back-splash and counters, including new flooring for entry, hall and kitchen, took this home to the next level for a maximum return on investment.
