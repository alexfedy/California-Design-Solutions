## Betty D. Kitchen Reno

Beautiful kitchen update in monochromatic color palette. We expanded the kitchen U shape 15" by adding two corner lazy Susan's left & right of the new range. Herringbone tile adds a layer of texture to complement the raised panel doors & honed counters echo the look of the smudge-proof appliances. The old cabinets over the peninsula were removed to see the beautiful view of the outside orchard!

Project Year: 2020

Project Cost: $50,001 - $75,000

Country: United States
