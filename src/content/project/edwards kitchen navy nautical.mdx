## EDWARDS KITCHEN - Navy Nautical

A tiny 50's bungalow is revamped with a top shelf collection of appliances and fixtures in this Sacramento remodel. Our client Mr. Edwards was ready for a bold change with colorful navy painted cabinets and a kitchen ready to perform efficiently with KitchenAid appliances. We removed one nook window to accommodate tall pantry cabs, wine refer and "X" wine storage and KitchenAid French door refer. The well organized kitchen layout provides excellent storage, multiple work areas and a place for kids to sit outside of the work triangle - all attained without removing any walls. Check out the Before pics!

Project Cost: $50,001 - $75,000
