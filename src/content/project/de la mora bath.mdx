## DE LA MORA BATH

Simple and modern was the directive for this pretty little bath, with emphasis on the custom vanity inset doors & drawers. A beautiful marble mosaic tile creates a stone "carpet" inset in front of the sleek vanity and polished quartz counter. All tile surfaces, counter and fixtures coordinate for a clean & bright bathroom.
