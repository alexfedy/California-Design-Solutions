## BRUNO MASTER SHOWER

Tub to shower conversion with large scale ceramic tile walls & floor. Chrome fixtures include mix valve, diverter, hand held wand on mounted bar, square wall mounted rain shower head & ceiling rain head. Custom 1/2" inch frame-less glass door & fixed panels.

Project Cost: $25,001 - $50,000

Country: United States
