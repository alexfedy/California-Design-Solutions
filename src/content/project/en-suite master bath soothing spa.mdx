## EN-SUITE MASTER BATH SOOTHING SPA COLORS

This was a wonderful transformation for our Davis California client. The biggest designer/client decisions were whether or not to completely remove the tub from the master bath. After many design options were considered, our client chose to remove the tub, since another bathroom had one, and expand the vanity area and enlarge the shower. The overall spa feel was accomplished using only 3 colors.

Project Year: 2020

Project Cost: $25,001 - $50,000

Country: United States
