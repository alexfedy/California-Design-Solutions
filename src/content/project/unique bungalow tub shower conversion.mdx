## UNIQUE BUNGALOW TUB/SHOWER CONVERSION - SACRAMENTO CA

This is one of my favorite projects! This tiny hall bath becomes a show stopping jewel box with a bold large format tile in a soft palette. Truly a delightful transformation from start to finish, with clients open to colorful, big, bold & beautiful & unafraid of a design risk. You'll see the giant patterned tile placed from shower wall to floor. A tall glass shower enclosure lets your eyes capture the entire space & a ceiling painted in a soft shade of sky blue tricks the mind into thinking the room is larger as well. The materials are bold & oversized, they are unified in scale and color & balanced to work perfectly with custom cabinets matching the rich colors of the hardwood floors throughout the rest of the home.

Project Year: 2022

Project Cost: $25,001 - $50,000

Country: United States
