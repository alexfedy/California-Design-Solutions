## PART 1 - WHOLE HOME UPDATE - KITCHEN, FIREPLACE, BATHROOMS, DOORS & MORE!

This project was a wonderful whole home remodel with a refreshing finish. The compartmentalized rooms were dark, dated, cramped & weren't conducive to the watchful care of our client's two charming daughters. Now the space is bright, open & superbly functional & offers several separate areas for homework, island dining, meal preparation, seated dining area, family and a warm new fireplace now enjoyed in the new open areas.
The new floor plan, kitchen design, neutral color palette and finishes will remain in style & appreciated for decades!

Project Year: 2022

Project Cost: $150,001 - $200,000
