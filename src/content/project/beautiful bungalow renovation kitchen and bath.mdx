## Beautiful Bungalow Renovation - Sacramento, CA

This enchanting West Sacramento Bungalow sits within a beautifully landscaped environment which was started 4 years before the Owner's focused their talented eye on renovating the interior kitchen & bath. The single bath was decked out in natural finishes reflecting their stunning backyard & fountain stream. Add to that Lux Spa features like the heated floor, custom floating vanity, wall-hung toilet, tall custom shower enclosure & raised shower fixtures, and you're on a fab staycation! This forever bath needed age-in-place functionality without a hint of institutional vibes. Stylish grab bars, including the TP holder accommodate any future mobility concerns. The kitchen's transformation was all about more counter & cabinet space. Finishes were combined to compliment the park-like view & soon a natural live edge slab will be fashioned into shelves & counter under the 2nd window. The new sink located under the raised windows doubled the counter space & organized the appliances. Another bonus for our tall clients was to raise the counters & appliances 3 inches - while standing on a more forgiving new hardwood floor.

Project Year: 2023

Project Cost: $100,001 - $150,000
