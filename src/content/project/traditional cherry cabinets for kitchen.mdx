## TRADITIONAL CHERRY CABINETS FOR KITCHEN & STAIRWELL UPDATE

Complete kitchen, family room, entry, laundry many other areas of this spacious home were designed with a nod to traditional styling using warm tones and raised panel cherry cabinets. The cabinets were used throughout the home including a custom TV entertainment center, all vanities & a large study desk.
Lots of textures and warm finishes for this traditional style create a space that looks bigger and more to scale with the tall ceilings.

Project Cost: $150,001 - $200,000
